An Econometric Analysis of the Mincer Wage Equation: The Association of Education and Experience with Wages in India
With Dhruv Aryan · Supervised by Dr. Benston John · Published on SSRN, May 2026

Estimates a semi-log Mincerian wage equation via OLS on 5,000 wage-employed individuals from the IHDS-II (2011–12) household survey, regressing log hourly wages on schooling, experience, experience-squared, gender, and urban location. Applies HC1 heteroskedasticity-robust standard errors.

Inflation Burden Across Income Groups: A Cross-Country Analysis of India, the United States, and Japan
Independent research · November 2025

Analyses 72 country-year observations across India, the United States, and Japan (2000–2023) using World Bank WDI data to assess how inflation burden differs across income groups, finding that higher inflation disproportionately erodes the income share and consumption growth of lower-income households.
